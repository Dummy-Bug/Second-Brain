## Functional Requirements

- **Hotel Homepage**
    
    - Browse featured hotels
        
    - Promotions and location discovery
        
- **Hotel Room Information**
    
    - Room types
        
    - Pricing
        
    - Amenities
        
    - Availability calendar
        
- **Reserve a Room**
    
    - Date selection
        
    - Guest details
        
    - Payment
        
    - Booking confirmation
        
- **Admin Capabilities**
    
    - Create rooms
        
    - Update pricing
        
    - Modify availability
        
    - Delete room listings
